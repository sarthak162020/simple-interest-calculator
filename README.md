\section*{Simple Interest Calculator}

\subsection*{Overview}
This project is a \textbf{Simple Interest Calculator} that computes the interest earned or payable on a principal amount over a fixed period of time at a given annual interest rate.

It helps users calculate the Simple Interest and Total Amount quickly without performing manual calculations.

\subsection*{Formula Used}

The formula for Simple Interest is:

\[
SI = \frac{P \times R \times T}{100}
\]

Where:
\begin{itemize}
    \item $P$ = Principal amount
    \item $R$ = Rate of interest (per annum)
    \item $T$ = Time (in years)
    \item $SI$ = Simple Interest
\end{itemize}

The Total Amount ($A$) is calculated as:

\[
A = P + SI
\]

\subsection*{Example Calculation}

Suppose:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Principal ($P$) = 10000
    \item Rate ($R$) = 5\%
    \item Time ($T$) = 2 years
\end{itemize}

Then,

\[
SI = \frac{10000 \times 5 \times 2}{100} = 1000
\]

Total Amount:

\[
A = 10000 + 1000 = 11000
\]

Therefore, the Simple Interest is 1000 and the total amount after 2 years is 11000.

\subsection*{How to Use}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Enter the Principal amount.
    \item Enter the Rate of Interest.
    \item Enter the Time period (in years).
    \item Click on the Calculate button.
    \item The calculator displays:
    \begin{itemize}
        \item Simple Interest
        \item Total Amount
    \end{itemize}
\end{enumerate}
